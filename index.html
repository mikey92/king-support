Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Where is my data stored?

All account and run data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore). Your data is associated with your account and deleted permanently when you delete your account.

Can I use KOR offline?

GPS tracking works offline — the app records your path locally during a run. However, uploading your run, viewing leaderboards, and syncing data require an internet connection.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account inside the app. This permanently deletes your profile, run history, territory data, and all associated data from our servers.

How does HealthKit integration work?

When you grant HealthKit permission, KOR can read workout sessions recorded by other apps (Garmin, Strava, WHOOP, etc.) and import them as runs. We never write to HealthKit and never share your health data with third parties.

How do I create or join a crew?

Open the Crew tab and tap Create Crew or search for an existing crew to join. Crew creators can manage members, approve join requests, and remove members at any time.

What counts as "territory"?

Territory is the total area (in square meters or km²) covered by your GPS paths. The more streets and paths you run through, the more territory you accumulate. Territory is virtual and represents no real-world property rights.

Can I change my display name?

Yes. Go to Settings → Profile to update your display name and profile picture at any time.

Why does KOR need location access?

Location is required to track your run path in real time and to map the territory you conquer. We only use location data during active runs and for building your territory map. Location data is never sold or shared with advertisers.

How do I report a bug or send feedback?

Email us at support@kingofrun.app or use the in-app feedback option under Settings → Contact Us.
